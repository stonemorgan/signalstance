## Carousel Content: Before/After Format

Generate structured content for a multi-slide {{platform.name}} carousel PDF showing side-by-side transformations.

### Output Format (strict — follow exactly)

TITLE: [Frame the transformation]
SUBTITLE: [Optional one-line subtitle]
PAIR 1 BEFORE: [Weak version — in quotes if applicable]
PAIR 1 AFTER: [Strong rewrite or improvement]
PAIR 1 NOTE: [1 sentence explaining the principle behind the change]
PAIR 2 BEFORE: [Weak version]
PAIR 2 AFTER: [Strong rewrite]
PAIR 2 NOTE: [1 sentence explanation]
...continue for 4-6 pairs...
CTA: [Specific call-to-action related to the topic]

### Rules

- Exactly 4-6 pairs
- Before examples must be realistic — things your audience actually does
- After examples must show concrete improvement
- Contrast must be striking and educational
- Notes explain the principle, not just the change
- Frame as strategy, not just editing
- CTA must be specific to the topic
